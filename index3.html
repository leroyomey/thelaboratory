Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 16, 2024

1. Introduction

At readEasy, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information while using the readEasy extension. By using the extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Saved Preferences: readEasy stores your font preference settings locally within your browser's storage. This data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension. We do not collect, access, or store this data on any external servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Locally Stored Data: Your font preferences are stored locally in your browser. This data is used exclusively to enable or disable dyslexia-friendly fonts and is not transmitted to any third party.

4. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

No Third-Party Access: We do not sell, share, or transfer any personal information or saved content with third parties. Your data remains private and under your control.

Legal Requirements: We may only disclose data if required by law or if necessary to protect our rights or respond to legal processes.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Although the data is stored locally in your browser, we recommend keeping your browser secure by using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication where available.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention: Your font preference is stored locally in your browser as long as the extension is installed.

Data Deletion: You can reset your font preferences by uninstalling the readEasy extension, which will also remove all locally stored data from your browser.

7. User Control

User Consent: By using readEasy, you consent to the local storage of your font preference and understand that no external parties will have access to it.

Opting Out: If you no longer wish to use the extension or store data, you can uninstall the extension, which will remove all associated data from your browser.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the extension’s functionality or legal requirements. When we make changes, the “Effective Date” will be updated. We encourage users to review the Privacy Policy periodically.
